About Us
Our Vision

To spread smiles and equality of resources - from the extremely abled to the differently abled.

Our Mission

HLF's mission has always been to spread smiles and in a way, use our privilege for the betterment of our country and our people. Our platform extends support to especially orphans and the underprivileged - by catering to their acquired talents including but not limited to craftwork, education, and more.

Our Team

We operate as a team who have proudly been a part of the happiness projects and every department from Human Resources to Public Relations, Finance to Logistics, Social Media to Graphics are crucial cogs in this running machine.
